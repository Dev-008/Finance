The Savings Planner helps you set and track financial goals:
1. Go to Dashboard â†’ Savings Planner

The Investment Calculator uses the compound interest formula to estimate your investment growth:
1. Enter your initial investment amount

5. Click "Calculate"

The calculator will show you the final amount and total interest earned.
